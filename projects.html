⇢ Chess Foundery

Chess Foundry is a hobby of mine designing digital chess sets and other chess related projects. You may ask, “Do we need more digital chess sets?” The answer is, “I suppose not really, but I don’t care.” Chess Foundry is about making digital chess sets that look good and are natural to use.
